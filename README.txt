dotdash, a jamBOX game.

code: michael carriere

description: two or four players, red vs. blue. two players are tied together by a bungie cord. hold bumper to break, trigger to dash, and grab five points to win! (green is +1 point, magenta is -1 point. grab the +1s and drag your enemies into -1s.)

requires: unity 4.3, xbox controller.
